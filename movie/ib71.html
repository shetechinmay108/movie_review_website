ib 71 review

"IB 71" is a gripping action thriller based on real events leading up to the Indo-Pakistan War of 1971. It follows intelligence officer Dev Jammwal (Vidyut Jammwal) as he orchestrates a daring operation to thwart a Pakistan-China attack on India by hijacking an Indian plane and landing it in Pakistani territory. The film focuses on the tension-filled mission, with impressive performances, especially by Vidyut and Vishal Jethwa as a Kashmiri separatist. The action sequences, cinematography, and historical accuracy stand out, though the loud background score detracts from the overall experience. With its intense pacing and absence of unnecessary songs, "IB 71" is an engaging watch for fans of period thrillers, offering a riveting portrayal of India's intelligence network.
