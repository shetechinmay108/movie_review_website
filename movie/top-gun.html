Top Gun review

*Top Gun: Maverick* brings back the iconic Tom Cruise as Maverick, now a 60-something dealing with guilt, redemption, and the consequences of aging. The film blends nostalgia with emotional depth, offering thrilling aerial stunts and high stakes. While it retains the flashy bravado of the original, it explores Maverick’s personal growth and challenges. Cruise’s performance is magnetic, and the aerial sequences are stunning. Though Val Kilmer’s brief appearance as Iceman is a highlight and Jennifer Connelly adds depth as Maverick’s love interest, the absence of Kelly McGillis and the new theme song don’t quite live up to the original. Despite a few far-fetched moments, *Top Gun: Maverick* is a worthy, adrenaline-fueled sequel.
